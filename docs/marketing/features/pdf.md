# Xplaino PDF — Product Marketing Features

> For use by AI marketing agents to generate landing page copy, email pitches, ad creatives, and sales messaging.

---

## Upload — Zero Friction to Get Started

- Upload a PDF in seconds via drag-and-drop, click-to-browse, or paste (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V) — three ways to get started
- No browser extension or desktop app required — works entirely in the browser
- Lands directly in the interactive PDF viewer the moment upload completes — no waiting, no extra steps
- Works for both logged-in users and guests, so there is no signup wall to try the product

---

## AI Chat with Your PDF

- Ask any question in plain English and get an instant, accurate answer sourced directly from the document
- Answers stream in real time — no waiting for the full response to generate
- Every AI answer includes numbered citation badges — click any citation to jump to the exact source passage in the PDF, highlighted in teal
- Suggested follow-up questions appear automatically after each answer, keeping the research flow going
- One-click built-in prompts: **Summarise** and **Key Takeaways** — extract value from any PDF in a single click
- Create multiple named chat sessions per PDF — run a legal analysis in one tab and a business summary in another, simultaneously
- All chat sessions and history are saved server-side — close the tab, come back later, and pick up exactly where you left off
- Stop any response mid-stream if you have what you need

---

## AI Actions on Any Selected Text

- Select any text in the PDF and an AI toolbar appears instantly — no menus to dig through
- One-click contextual AI actions directly on selected text:
  - **Explain** — breaks down complex language into plain terms
  - **Summarize** — condenses a passage into 3–4 sentences
  - **Key Points** — extracts the main ideas as a list
  - **Convert to Bullet Points** — restructures dense paragraphs into scannable bullets
  - **Legal Implications** — surfaces key legal considerations from any clause or section
  - **Research Gaps** — identifies open questions and areas for further investigation
  - **Create Study Notes** — auto-generates concise study material from selected content
- **Ask AI free-form** — send any selected text directly into the chat as context and ask anything

---

## Custom AI Prompts — Build Your Personal AI Toolkit

- Save any AI prompt you use repeatedly as a named custom prompt — accessible in one click every time
- Custom prompts appear in both the text-selection toolbar and the chat panel for maximum reach
- Share custom prompts with teammates — send a prompt to any user by their ID
- Receive prompts shared by colleagues — build a shared team prompt library
- Full CRUD: create, edit, delete, show/hide prompts as your workflow evolves

---

## Highlights — Mark What Matters

- Highlight any text in the PDF with a single click after selecting it
- Choose from multiple colours to visually categorise information (e.g. by theme, priority, or topic)
- Colour preference is remembered — your default colour is saved to your profile
- Highlights are anchored to the actual text content, not pixel positions — they survive page reloads, zoom changes, and re-opens
- Hover any existing highlight to edit the colour or remove it

---

## Notes & Annotations — Add Your Own Context

- Attach a personal note to any selected text or existing highlight
- Notes open as a floating, resizable editor positioned next to the annotated text
- The editor repositions automatically as you scroll — stays in view without covering the content
- A note icon appears in the page margin at the exact line of each note for fast visual scanning
- Full CRUD: create, update, delete notes at any time

---

## Page Translation — Read Any PDF in Your Language

- Translate every page of any PDF into your native language with a single click
- Uses Chrome's on-device AI translator when available — instant, zero network latency, fully private
- Falls back to a backend translation service for non-Chrome or older browsers
- Language is auto-detected from the document; your preferred native language is pre-loaded from your profile
- Toggle between the original and translated view at any time — side-by-side comparison workflow

---

## Sharing & Collaboration — Share With Anyone, Control Who Sees What

- Share any PDF with specific people by entering their email address — no account required for the recipient to view
- Get a shareable link in one click — copy and send anywhere
- Toggle access between **Private** (invite-only) and **Public** (anyone with the link) at any time
- Public PDFs can be shared directly to LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Facebook, and WhatsApp in one click — perfect for publishing research, reports, or thought-leadership content
- Manage your sharees list — see everyone who has access and revoke access per person instantly
- Viewers of a public PDF can make a private copy to their own account and annotate it independently — no need to re-upload

---

## Organisation — Keep Your PDF Library Clean

- Organise PDFs into nested folders — mirror your existing file system or project structure
- Sidebar folder switcher and PDF switcher let you navigate your entire library without leaving the viewer
- Switch between PDFs in the same folder without going back to the dashboard
- Download the original PDF file at any time from within the viewer

---

## First-Time Experience — Instant Feature Discovery

- A guided 2-step overlay appears on the very first open, spotlighting the toolbar and explaining how to use AI and highlights — zero learning curve, zero support needed
- Discovery state is saved so returning users never see it again

---

## Target Use Cases (for messaging context)

- **Students & academics** — Chat with research papers, generate study notes, highlight key sections, translate foreign-language sources
- **Legal professionals** — Surface legal implications from contracts, annotate clauses, share documents securely with colleagues
- **Knowledge workers & consultants** — Extract key takeaways from reports, summarise lengthy documents, build custom prompt workflows
- **Content creators & publishers** — Read and annotate sources, share PDFs publicly with a social link
- **Teams** — Collaborate on the same document via shared access, shared highlights, and shared custom prompts
